What is Carbon Footprint

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, including carbon dioxide and methane, that are generated by our actions. The average individualâ€™s carbon footprint includes a wide variety of activities such as driving a car, using electricity, and the food we eat.

Effects on the Environment

Greenhouse gases, emitted through our daily activities, trap heat in the atmosphere and lead to climate change. This results in global warming, melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and extreme weather conditions, affecting ecosystems and biodiversity.
